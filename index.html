Supporting Growth, Empowering Futures

At Discovery Place, our mission is to provide high-quality Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy services that empower children with autism to reach their full potential. We work alongside families to create a supportive and personalized path to growth, development, and lasting success.

Our Services

Early Intervention ABA

Intensive therapy designed for young children, focusing on foundational skills to support developmental milestones and prepare for future learning.

School-Based ABA Therapy

In-school support that helps children integrate and succeed in educational environments, fostering both academic and social growth.

Home-Based ABA Therapy

Therapy sessions conducted in the child’s home to support skill development within their daily routines, promoting comfort and consistency.

Social Skills Training

Structured guidance to build communication and social interaction skills, helping children connect positively with others.

Life Skills Training

Focused on practical, everyday skills such as self-care and independence, enabling children to navigate their surroundings confidently.

Parent Training

Providing families with strategies, tools, and resources to reinforce their child’s progress and create a supportive home environment.

Our Core Values
